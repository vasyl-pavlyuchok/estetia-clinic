# QA Checklist (Estetia)

## High Severity

- Broken critical navigation or CTA flow.
- Invalid service data source (not coming from `services.json`).
- Strong visual/brand drift from brief constraints.
- Accessibility blockers (missing focus paths, unreadable contrast).

## Medium Severity

- Inconsistent spacing or typography scale.
- Motion too abrupt or distracting.
- Unclear hierarchy in key sections (Hero, IA showcase, Services).
- Copy with vague claims or weak clarity.

## Low Severity

- Minor alignment or spacing polish gaps.
- Slight inconsistency in icon/button styles.
- Non-critical microinteraction rough edges.

## Final Pass Questions

1. Does this feel intentionally premium, not template-like?
2. Is section purpose understandable within 3 seconds?
3. Are data and claims accurate to project sources?
4. Is mobile experience as coherent as desktop?
5. Did changes preserve or improve quality (no downgrade)?
