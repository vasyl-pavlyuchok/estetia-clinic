# Build Gates (Estetia)

## Gate 1: Scope

- Section objective is explicit.
- Dependencies and files to touch are identified.
- No conflict with brief constraints.

## Gate 2: Data

- Service data comes from `/_project/services.json`.
- No invented treatments, categories, prices, or claims.

## Gate 3: Visual

- Premium minimal look preserved.
- Clear hierarchy (headline, support text, CTA).
- At least one deliberate focus mechanism (isolation/contrast).

## Gate 4: Motion

- Motion supports comprehension, not decoration.
- Durations are soft (roughly 200-700ms depending on context).
- No heavy effects that hurt performance.

## Gate 5: Technical

- Reusable components over duplication.
- No unnecessary external dependencies.
- Works on mobile and desktop.
